I am working as an Machine Learning Engineer at Engineerbabu working on an intersection of computer vision, biomedical and web development. Before that I completed my bachelors in computer science at Medicaps University. My research interest lies broadly in computer vision and bioinformatics. Much of my work includes image classification coupled with computer aided diagnosis and image generation. Problems in this vein that I'm currently excited about include:

(1) Assistive and Guided surgery using computer vision
(2) Predicting mutation on chronic diseases using adversarial nets
